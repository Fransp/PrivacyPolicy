Last updated: April 16, 2026

Try Balalay Games (Individual Entrepreneur Igor Kliukin) acts as the data controller for personal data collected through this app. Third-party services listed in Section 5 act as data processors on our behalf.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data through our third-party SDKs:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)

  • Precise location (GPS) – collected by certain third-party SDKs for analytics and marketing purposes
  • App interactions (in-game events, session data, progression)

  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

3. Purposes of Data Collection

  • Analytics – to understand user behaviour and improve app performance
  • Advertising and marketing – to deliver ads and measure ad performance

For users in the European Economic Area and other applicable jurisdictions, we process data on the following legal bases:
