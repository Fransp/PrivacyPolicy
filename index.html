Privacy Policy for Raccoon Blast

Last updated: April 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and disclosed when you use the mobile game Raccoon Blast.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device identifiers (e.g. IDFA/GAID)
  • Approximate and precise location
  • App interactions
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

2. Purposes of Data Collection

  • Advertising and marketing – to deliver personalized ads and track ad performance.
  • Analytics – to understand user behavior and improve app performance.
  • Diagnostics – to detect crashes and technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party SDKs and services, which may collect data as described above:

  • Facebook SDK
  • AppLovin
  • Amplitude
  • Yandex AppMetrica
  • Unity In-App Purchasing
  • Google AdMob

These providers may have access to certain personal data under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.

4. Data Security

Please note that:

  • Data is not encrypted in transit.
  • Data is not transferred over a secure connection.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

At this time, we do not provide a way to request data deletion. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Children's Privacy

This game is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.
